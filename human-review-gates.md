# Human Review Gates For Local-Service AI Workflows

Human review gates are the control points that keep AI-assisted workflows useful
without letting them make sensitive customer or money decisions alone.

## Actions AI Can Prepare

- customer summaries
- intake questions
- first-response drafts
- quote follow-up drafts
- routing recommendations
- support triage notes
- weekly report drafts

## Actions That Stay Reviewed

- pricing
- dispatch
- refunds
- account changes
- payment-sensitive actions
- warranty decisions
- legal or medical guidance
- unusual customer commitments

This is the main distinction between TeamShift's reviewed AI operations model
and an unsupervised chatbot or generic automation.
