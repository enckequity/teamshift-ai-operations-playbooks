## Short Description

TeamShift provides done-for-you AI operations and website workflows for small
businesses in Central Pennsylvania and across the United States.

## Service-Area Description

TeamShift serves Lancaster, Harrisburg, York, and nearby Central Pennsylvania
businesses remotely as a service-area provider. TeamShift does not operate a
public walk-in office in those cities.

## Full Description

TeamShift helps owner-led teams recover missed calls, follow up on quotes, route
web leads, prepare customer handoffs, clean up customer operations, and keep
pricing, dispatch, refunds, and customer commitments under human review.

TeamShift is not primarily an MSP or cybersecurity provider. TeamShift focuses
on practical customer operations and workflow execution for small businesses
that need work completed without managing another software tool.
