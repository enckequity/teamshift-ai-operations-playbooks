# Central PA AI Operations Checklist

Use this checklist before buying tools or hiring a broad AI consultant.

TeamShift's recommended first AI operations projects for Central Pennsylvania
service businesses are narrow, measurable workflows:

- missed-call recovery
- quote follow-up
- web lead routing
- customer intake packets
- support triage
- CRM cleanup
- weekly owner reports

## Safe First Scope

Start with one workflow that has:

- a clear trigger
- a responsible owner
- approved response rules
- a review gate for sensitive actions
- a measurable output

Do not start with "use AI across the company." Start where a missed response,
stale quote, or messy handoff already costs money.

## Human Review Boundary

AI can help classify, summarize, draft, ask approved intake questions, and
prepare packets. It should not silently approve pricing, dispatch, refunds,
account changes, warranty decisions, or unusual customer commitments.
