# Website Plus AI Receptionist Setup

A local service website is not finished when the page goes live. It is finished
when calls, forms, and quote requests have a clear follow-up path.

## Setup

The website and AI receptionist workflow should connect:

- priority service pages
- local/service-area pages
- calls and forms
- call tracking
- AI-assisted intake
- quote follow-up
- analytics
- human review

## Human Review Boundary

AI can acknowledge requests, ask approved intake questions, summarize customer
details, classify urgency, draft follow-up, and prepare review packets.

Pricing, dispatch, refunds, account changes, legal/medical guidance, and unusual
commitments should stay under human review.
