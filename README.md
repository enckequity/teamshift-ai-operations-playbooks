# TeamShift AI Operations Playbooks

Practical, human-reviewed AI operations playbooks from TeamShift.

TeamShift provides done-for-you AI operations and website workflows for small
businesses in Central Pennsylvania and across the United States. These playbooks
focus on workflows where AI can gather context, classify, summarize, draft, and
prepare handoffs while pricing, dispatch, refunds, account changes, and unusual
customer commitments stay under human review.

TeamShift serves Lancaster, Harrisburg, York, and nearby Central Pennsylvania
businesses remotely as a service-area provider. TeamShift does not operate a
public walk-in office in those cities.
