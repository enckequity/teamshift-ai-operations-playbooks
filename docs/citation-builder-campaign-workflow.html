Citation builder campaign workflow

TeamShift uses this workflow to help customers turn a citation audit from Citation Tracker, BrightLocal, Whitespark, Semrush, or a similar tool into a managed local listing campaign.

The workflow keeps the customer's canonical business profile under human approval, prepares directory submission packets, tracks claim and verification status, and reports back after listings are live or ready for rescan.

When to use it

  • A citation report shows missing key citations.
  • Existing listings have name, address, or phone errors.
  • A customer wants a repeatable local SEO listing campaign.
  • Paid, claimed, or account-owned directories need review.

Customer intake

Before submissions start, TeamShift confirms the canonical business name, address or service-area preference, zip or post code, phone number, website, category, hours, service areas, logo, approved photos, and account owner for each directory.

Human review gates

  • Approve the canonical name, address, and phone profile.
  • Approve any paid directory or membership decision.
  • Approve account logins, claims, and ownership changes.
  • Approve whether a street address should be hidden or published.
  • Approve final descriptions before submission.

Workflow output

Each customer receives a reviewed citation packet with the source report date, citation score at intake, status counts, prioritized directories, listing URLs, verification requests, blocked directories, paid decisions, and target rescan date.
